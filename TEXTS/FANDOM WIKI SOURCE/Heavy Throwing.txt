Heavy Throwing
EDIT
The ranged attack option for Berserkers. Heavy throwing weapons add some variety to the Berserker's lineup and can be used when enemies are resistant to melee damage.

DR HEAVY WEAPONS
Weapon Types
Boulders
Throws a huge boulder at a single target. Boulders have a long cooldown and are useful for taking down enemies with massive life bars. Boulders have the biggest power level of all weapons.

Boomerangs
Also called Battlerangs. Allows the Berserker to throw a lighter chakram. Each throw takes a bit of mana, though the attack is combo-able. The Charge attack makes an orbiting chakram that will damage enemies it comes in contact with.
