EDIT
There are 4 types of Dragon Charm artifacts:

Bleed Charm
It causes moderate bleeding damage overtime, with a 5% increase for every enemy you hit, capping at around 50%.

Attack Power-up Charm
With each enemy you strike, your overall damage increases by 10%, reaching a maximum 100% attack bonus, effectively doubling your damage output.

Defense Power-up Charm
Increases your defense the same way as the attack power up charm.

Speed Charm
Speed charms increase (max capping at) 50% attack, buster generation and movement speed.
